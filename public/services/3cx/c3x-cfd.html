Frequently Asked Questions

Can callflows be changed after deployment?

Absolutely. CFD projects can be edited, refined, and re-uploaded at any time to adapt to your evolving business needs.

Do I need a specific 3CX license?

Authoring and running CFD applications requires a 3CX PRO or Enterprise license.

Can CFD access external databases?

Yes, through dedicated database components or standard HTTP requests (JSON/XML).
