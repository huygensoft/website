Predictive & Power Dialers

Enhance outbound calling with smart dialers that analyse agent availability and call lists to maximise connect rates.

Features

  • Predictive algorithm to dial numbers ahead of agent availability.
  • Power dialer mode for sequential calling.
  • CRM integration for call outcome logging and contact lookup.
  • Customisable pacing, drop‑call handling, and retries.
  • Reporting dashboards with call statistics.

Benefits

  • Maximises agent talk time and productivity.
  • Reduces idle time between calls.
  • Improves campaign efficiency and ROI.

Service Tiers & Add‑Ons

Dialer projects can be delivered as starter, standard or advanced packages. Optional features like custom pacing algorithms and reporting dashboards are available.
