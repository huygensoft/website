Custom CRM Applications

We develop and integrate custom CRM systems that work hand‑in‑hand with 3CX, providing real‑time call data, logging and user interfaces.

Features

  • Real‑time call logging and contact lookup within CRM.
  • Click‑to‑dial and call pop notifications.
  • Integration of CRM events with 3CX call flows.
  • Custom dashboards and reporting tools.

Benefits

  • Unified view of customer interactions.
  • Improved agent productivity and tracking.
  • Custom workflows encapsulating telephony and CRM data.

Service Tiers & Add‑Ons

CRM solutions are offered as modular packages, from basic call logging to fully custom dashboards. Add‑ons include AI summaries, calendar sync, and mobile CRM apps.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can the CRM work offline? Yes, we can implement local caching and sync whenever connectivity returns.
  • Is data encrypted? All call logs and personal data are encrypted at rest and in transit.
